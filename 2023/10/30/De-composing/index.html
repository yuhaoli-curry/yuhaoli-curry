(De)composing

Story

Russian composer Gregor Ivanovich has lived a life of musical mediocrity, never reaching his aspirations of creating a truly great work. As he nears the ending of his newest symphony, he has a brilliant epiphany - and then dies before he can write a single note. His unfulfilled spirit now wanders the symphony hall, using a menagerie of instruments to communicate his ideas to the student completing his work.

Description

(De)composing is a 2D puzzle platformer with elements of rhythm games. It combines the visceral action of platforming and rhythmic timing with the thoughtful rumination of puzzle-solving. The experience will be elevated by aesthetic and auditory flavor, as well as a premise rooted in gallows humor. The game will be especially enjoyed by fans of casual puzzle games or lovers of music, though it will also serve as a great introduction to either.
