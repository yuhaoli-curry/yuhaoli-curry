Empty Space

Description

Empty Space is a single-player First-Person Sci-Fi Horror Puzzle Game set on a spaceship sometime in the distant future. The main character the player controls is a new crew member who just arrived on the space station moments before a catastrophic event causes him to find himself waking up in a cryo pod, with the entire rest of the crew having seemingly vanished. Unaware of how this happened, he is left searching for the truth.
