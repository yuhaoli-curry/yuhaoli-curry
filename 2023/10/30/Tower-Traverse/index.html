Tower Traverse

Description

Tower Traverse is a 2D causal single player top down maze exploration game where the objective is to find a key in each level and bring it to the exit to move on. Navigate the darkness and dodge the many traps within the walls of the tower to stay alive! Do you have what it takes to make it to the top?
